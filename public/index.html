Uncertainty Swap: An Interactive Exhibit

Welcome to Uncertainty Swap, an interactive exhibit where people can share and swap experiences of uncertainty. This project is a journey into the complexities of decision-making in a world brimming with choices. Uncertainty is an exploration of indecision, magnified by the myriad of options presented by technology, pre-populated with personal anecdotes, family and friend's stories, and online forum posts. Uncertainty Swap is a space to share your own remarks on uncertainty, and in return, gain insight from many universally shared uncertain experiences.
